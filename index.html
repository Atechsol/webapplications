About Us

AWAAZ 2.0 MESCE is the official students' union of MES College of Engineering for the term of 2023 to 2024. Dedicated to addressing student concerns and fostering a supportive environment, AWAAZ 2.0 aims to empower students through guidance and advocacy. The union offers a comprehensive platform for student growth and development by

  • Providing workshops and technical sessions to enhance students' knowledge base.
  • Organizing cultural programs and other activities to promote holistic development.
  • Equipping students with the necessary skills to excel in their professional lives.

By facilitating these activities, AWAAZ 2.0 strives to create an enriching college experience, ensuring that students have the resources and support they need to thrive academically and socially.

Our Mission, Vision, and Goals

AWAAZ 2.0 aims to empower students through guidance and advocacy.

Our Mission

To empower MES College of Engineering students by providing a platform for holistic development and creating a supportive community.

Our Vision

To be the leading student union, shaping a transformative college experience characterized by academic excellence, personal growth, and social responsibility.

Our Goals

Championing student interests and addressing their concerns. Offering a diverse range of workshops and programs to enhance students' knowledge and abilities.

Our Values

Student-focused, inclusive, transparent, accountable, and striving for excellence.
